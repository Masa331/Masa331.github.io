Hi, i'm Premysl Donat. I'm software developer and entrepreneur from Prague, Czech Republic. I was born at 1990 and spent my childhood in small rural town. I attended technical high school which gave me a few good friends and a short-term tech aversion. That's why aftrwards i applied to law school. I didn't get in and as my father died around the same time i started working in a family business.

Family business wasn't much fun so i started to look elsewhere. I came out with a business idea for an online copywriting marketplace. Having no skills but some money i contracted small software agency. That was all right but somehow slow and in the meantime i started to learn programming myself. First some HTML and other web basics, then finally Ruby on Rails. And this really changed my life. Ruby is a beautiful beautiful language and ecosystem which fits perfectly with my beliefs and attitudes i consider myself very lucky that i came across it.

The marketplace wasn't a success and i quickly run out of money. Then i applied at UOL at junior Ruby developer position and i stayed for six great years and learned a great bunch not just about programming. Afterwards i spent two years at TopMonks. And this gets us almost to today. Now i run my own software development company Imagine Anything and also an accounting company TaxBox which i co-own with my former colleagues.

In my free time i enjoy rollerblading, reading and other normal things people usually do. Currently i'm not active on any social media except Github.

You can reach me at pdonat@seznam.cz.

Have a good one!
